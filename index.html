// why risk sentinel

Built on principles your auditors will recognize.

01

Deterministic, not a black box

// detection

Six detection rules, live today.

Each rule is customer-scoped and threshold-driven. Rules are transparent source you can audit — and new ones ship the same way.

LargeTransfer

Token transfers above a per-token, per-chain threshold.

Governance

Ownership transfers, role grants/revokes, pause / unpause, and proxy upgrades on your watched contracts.

MintBurn

Mint or burn events above your per-token limits.

Approval

Large ERC-20 approvals — and infinite (uint256.max) approvals.

BridgeTransfer

Large transfers into known cross-chain bridge contracts.

OracleUpdate

Chainlink price-feed moves beyond a basis-point threshold between updates.

// strongest coverage is on attacks that begin with an on-chain administrative action — ownership changes, role grants, upgrades — which leave a detectable signal before funds move, giving you lead time to respond.
